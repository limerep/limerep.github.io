Who am I?

Great question!

I'm passionate about making things look and sound awesome and loves exploring new ways of doing so. Constantly on the hunt for new challenges I recently discovered the joy of programming, and quickly realized that I had stumbled across a whole new dimension of expressing my creativity. Growing up playing videogames for as many hours a day as was humanly possible I've allways had a special place in my heart for the incredible pieces of art that they are. It took me a while to realize that I myself could be involved in actually creating them, and not just enjoying the work of others. This is as far as I've come on this journey towards becoming a professional game developer.

Having played various instruments (mostly drums) since i was 8 and worked with music production software for roughly 5 years I am ready for most tasks concerning audio for your projects and you can check out my SoundCloud account to hear a fraction of the pieces I've written and produced.

Thanks to my amazing little brother I have extensive eperience of working, assisting and communicating with humans of all ages with various disabilities. This has taught me not only the values of co-operation and communication in groups which is essential in agile software development teams, but also that we humans come in all forms and sizes and that there is allways room for self-improvement & learning!
